## Should we add a 3% daily loss circuit breaker to the forex trading bot?
**Date:** 2026-03-01T22:29:03
**Decision:** ### Synthesis of Board Contributions

#### Analysis
The proposed 3% daily loss circuit breaker is a critical risk management tool that aligns well with both operational and financial objectives. The CRO highlighted the importance of protecting capital while minimizing the impact on trading opportunities, and the CTO emphasized technical feasibility and maintainability.

#### Building On Previous
- **CRO**: Emphasized the need for continuous monitoring and compliance.
- **CTO**: Highlighted the importance of modular design, stress testing, and performance optimization.

#### Recommendations

1. **Implement a 3% Daily Loss Circuit Breaker**:
   - Introduce a circuit breaker that triggers when cumulative losses exceed 3% in a single day.
   - Ensure it is implemented as a separate module to maintain clean code and ease of maintenance.

2. **Monitor and Adjust**:
   - Continuously monitor the bot’s performance during both London and Asian sessions.
   - Document all changes and decisions made regarding the circuit breaker for compliance purposes.

3. **Technical Implementation**:
   - Develop a modular risk management component that integrates seamlessly with existing systems.
   - Conduct stress testing under various market conditions to ensure reliability.
   - Implement comprehensive logging and monitoring tools to track circuit breaker actions in real-time.

4. **Compliance and Documentation**:
   - Ensure all aspects of the circuit breaker are compliant with financial regulations and terms of service.
   - Document compliance measures and maintain a clear audit trail.

5. **Performance Optimization**:
   - Optimize the circuit breaker’s performance to avoid false triggers and ensure it does not impact trading during peak hours.
   - Use configuration settings for easy toggling and adjustments as needed.

6. **Proof-of-Concept (PoC)**:
   - Develop a PoC to simulate the circuit breaker's functionality under different market conditions before full deployment.
   - Test its effectiveness in both normal and volatile market scenarios.

#### Risks/Concerns
- **False Triggers**: Ensure proper calibration of the circuit breaker to avoid false positives.
- **Performance Bottlenecks**: Optimize the implementation to prevent any performance issues during peak trading times.
