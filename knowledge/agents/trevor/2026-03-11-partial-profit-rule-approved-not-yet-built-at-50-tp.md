## 📝 Partial profit rule approved (not yet built): at 50% TP distance close 50% of position, move SL to breakeven
**Date:** 2026-03-11T14:21:57
**Type:** note
**Workspace:** workspaces/forex-trading-team
**Tags:** partial-profit, guardian, risk-management, pending-build

Tim's rule: never lose if it's in profit — capture it. Both 2026-03-11 trades peaked well short of TP (GBP +10.4p, TP 12.8p away; NZD +3.7p, TP 5.2p away) then reversed before guardian could act. Partial profit plan: when position reaches 50% of distance to TP, close half the position and move SL to BE. This locks in profit even if trade reverses before full TP.

**Evidence:** GBP peaked +10.4p at 81% to TP, reversed to -.40. NZD peaked +3.7p at 71% to TP, reversed to -.50. Both guardian closes were correct but partial profit would have banked ~-4 on each.
