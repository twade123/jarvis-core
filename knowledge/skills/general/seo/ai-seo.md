## Skill Reference
### AI Search Platform Behaviors

**Google AI Overviews:** Heavily favors top 3 traditional search results, prioritizes content with clear factual statements and lists.

**ChatGPT/Perplexity:** Prefer authoritative domains with recent, well-structured content and clear source attribution.

**Claude/Gemini:** Focus on comprehensive, well-cited content with logical information hierarchy.

### Content Structure for AI Extraction

**Lead with answers, not buildup:**
- Weak: "Many factors contribute to X, but after extensive research, we found..."  
- Strong: "X is caused by three primary factors: [list]. Here's why..."

**Use extractable formatting:**
- Numbered lists for processes
- Bullet points for features/benefits  
- Tables for comparisons
- Headers that directly answer questions

### Query Intent Optimization

**Target question-based queries:** AI systems prioritize content that directly answers "what is," "how to," "why does," and "which" queries.

**Match semantic clusters:**
- Don't just optimize for "email marketing"
- Include: "email campaigns," "newsletter strategy," "automated email sequences"
- AI systems understand context better than exact-match keywords

### Citation-Friendly Markup

**Essential structured data:**
- FAQ schema for question-answer pairs
- Article schema with author/publish date
- Organization schema for credibility signals

**Internal linking for authority:**
- Link to supporting statistics and studies
- Reference other authoritative content on your domain
- Use descriptive anchor text, not "click here"

### Content Depth Anti-Patterns

**Avoid thin comparison content:**
- Bad: "Tool A vs Tool B: Both are good options"
- Good: Specific feature comparisons, pricing analysis, use-case recommendations with reasoning

**Don't bury key information:**
- AI systems extract from the first 2-3 paragraphs
- Place primary value proposition and key facts early
- Use the "inverted pyramid" journalism structure

### Performance Tracking

**Monitor these queries:**
- "[your brand] vs [competitor]"
- "what is [your product category]"
- "best [your product category]"
- "how to [primary use case]"

**Check citation frequency:**
- Search your brand name in ChatGPT, Perplexity monthly
- Track Google AI Overviews appearance for target queries
- Monitor competitor citations to identify content gaps

### Authority Signals AI Systems Recognize

**Publication credibility indicators:**
- Author bylines with credentials
- Clear publication/update dates
- External citations to authoritative sources
- Domain expertise demonstrated through content depth

**Technical authority markers:**
- SSL certificates and site security
- Fast loading speeds (affects crawling)
- Mobile optimization
- Clean site architecture
